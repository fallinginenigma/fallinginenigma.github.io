## [Cold Start Problem by Andrew Chen](https://a16z.com/book/the-cold-start-problem/)

My first basketball shoes were a pair of Nikes and they were the best pair of shoes I ever had. I loved the product, it wasn't cheap and I didn't understand why they were so expensive then. I wanted to know all about Nike's inception. I enjoyed reading this memoir. I could feel the pure joy of knowing how a man decided to sell what he believes in: running.
