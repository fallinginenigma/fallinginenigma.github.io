## About Work (goes to a new page)
I work at Grasim, it is Aditya Birla Group’s flagship company and it partly also owns UltraTech cement and Aditya Birla Capital. You might not have heard Grasim if you were born after the 2000s. It is a B2B brand called Birla Cellulose that manufactures Viscose Rayon Fibre, a man-made fibre similar to cotton.

It is ABG's oldest company, more than 75 years old and how the Birla’s built their empire and fortune from a small town called Nagda in Madhya Pradesh, India. Mostly caters to spinners that weave yarn and other textile companies that are clustered in Rajasthan and Tamil Nadu.
